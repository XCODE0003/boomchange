What is cryptocurrency, and how does it work?

 

Cryptography is a tool that is used by cryptocurrency to provide secure and private transactions. Cryptography refers to the use of encryption algorithms to encode and decode data. In simpler terms, cryptocurrency is a type of digital currency that operates independently of a central bank.

 

The two most popular types of cryptocurrencies are Bitcoin and Ethereum, but there are many others available on the market. Cryptocurrencies are bought and sold on exchanges, and the value of each currency is determined by supply and demand in the marketplace.
